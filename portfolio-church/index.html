God Reigns
Christian Ministry

Branding & Identity Design
Jul 2019

God Regins Christian Ministry is a new
church that was in need of branding
direction. I was tasked with refreshing &
focusing their branding.

The Brief

It was time to do God’s work! The client wanted a modern, stylish and trustworthy brand aesthetic. This was primarily a logo design but as the the church is a new one, I decided to provide them with a style guide they could grow into.

The Approach

As a result of the sit down with the client, I had a few set ideas regarding the logo design, colour scheme and typography that would best realise the client’s vision.

Logo:The logo is a combination of plain text and vibrant stained glass images arranged to form the outline of a cross. The end product is minimalist and visually engaging.

Colour Scheme:The visuals are set against a white background so that the elements pop. The main colour I picked for the text is teal-blue: renewal and clarity of thought. Then grey was used as the accent colour.

Typography:Serif and Sans Serif are the fonts of choice. The serif font, Escrow is the main font. It is authoritative, trustworthy, grand and respectable. The small text font is Tachyon, it is clean, legible, stylish and honest.

The End Product

This project afforded me the opportunity to say to my family, “I did God’s work”. It was a fun process and the end product is something that brought a smile to everyone's faces.
